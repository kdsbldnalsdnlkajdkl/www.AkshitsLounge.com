Server Rules

It is our mission to make A Minute With Mintu a safe and welcoming place for all. Every day, alot of people from around the world come together and express themselves on A Minute With Mintu. It is our goal to ensure all users have a valuable, inclusive and welcoming experience. These rules outline what is and isn't allowed on our Discord Server.

Preface

All rules in the server apply at all times and in all places. Most of the time, broken rules result in a warning - these expire with time and add up to punishments such as a mute or ban. Certain rules that are broken may come with an immediate ban.
Regarding Dank Memer and other currency bots - the soliciting of bot coins or items is not allowed as this results in a large amount of scamming, please move your discussion regarding this to DMs or consider doing it in an official server with these currency bots instead.
Propagating or influencing those that have broken the rules may result in punishment. This includes attempting to bait people into admitting they're under 13, trying to make people spam ping you through a nickname, or quoting/replying to rule-breaking messages.
Not all rules in the server are listed on this page, read the channel topic and pins of each channel to view any channel-specific rules that may be present. Please use common sense and avoid finding "loopholes" in the rules.
For moderation purposes, this server is English only. This means you cannot use ASCII, binary, morse code, ciphered code, or made-up languages from games/movies.
Copying any part of A Minute With Mintu including the rules, icons or banners is not allowed and will result in a permanent, unappealable ban.
We must also uphold Discord's Terms of Service, Community Guidelines and Partner Code of Conduct.
